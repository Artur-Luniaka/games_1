Returns & Refunds

Our customer-friendly return and refund policy

Digital Game Returns

Important: Digital game codes are non-refundable once delivered, as per our terms of service and platform policies.

Digital games cannot be returned because:

  • Codes are instantly delivered and cannot be deactivated
  • Once redeemed, codes are permanently linked to your account
  • Platform policies (Xbox, Steam, etc.) prohibit returns of activated codes
  • Digital content is considered "used" immediately upon delivery

Exception: If you receive a faulty or duplicate code, we will provide a replacement code within 24 hours.

Physical Game Returns

Physical games and merchandise can be returned within 30 days of delivery, subject to the following conditions:

Return Conditions

  • Time Limit: 30 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Must be in original, unopened condition
  • Packaging: Original packaging must be intact
  • Proof of Purchase: Order confirmation or receipt required

What Can Be Returned

  • Unopened physical games
  • Defective physical games (with proof of defect)
  • Wrong game received (we'll cover return shipping)
  • Damaged during shipping (we'll cover return shipping)
  • Unopened merchandise and accessories

What Cannot Be Returned

  • Opened physical games (unless defective)
  • Digital game codes (once delivered)
  • Downloaded content or DLC
  • Used or damaged merchandise
  • Personalized or custom items

Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact Us: Email returns@VirtuPlayMartZone.com or call +61 2 9955 7139
  2. Provide Details: Order number, item details, and reason for return
  3. Get Approval: We'll review your request and provide return authorization
  4. Ship Item: Send the item back using the provided return label or instructions
  5. Processing: We'll process your return within 5-7 business days of receipt
  6. Refund: Refund will be issued to your original payment method

Refund Information

Refund Timeline

  • Processing Time: 5-7 business days after we receive your return
  • Bank Processing: 3-10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • Total Time: 8-17 business days from return receipt

Refund Amount

  • Full Refund: Original purchase price (excluding shipping)
  • Shipping Cost: Return shipping is free for defective items or our errors
  • Restocking Fee: No restocking fees for valid returns

Refund Methods

  • Credit Card: Refunded to original card
  • PayPal: Refunded to PayPal account
  • Afterpay: Refunded through Afterpay system
  • Store Credit: Available upon request

Defective Items

If you receive a defective physical game:

  • Contact Us: Within 30 days of delivery
  • Provide Evidence: Photos or video of the defect
  • Return Shipping: We'll provide a prepaid return label
  • Replacement: We'll send a new copy or provide a refund
  • Warranty: Covered under manufacturer warranty

Note: Defective digital codes will be replaced with new codes within 24 hours.

Warranty Information

Physical Games

  • Manufacturer Warranty: 12 months from purchase date
  • Coverage: Manufacturing defects only
  • Exclusions: Damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear
  • Process: Contact us for warranty claims

Digital Games

  • Platform Warranty: Subject to Xbox, Steam, or other platform policies
  • Code Issues: We guarantee code functionality for 30 days
  • Replacement: Faulty codes replaced free of charge

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs depend on the reason for return:

Free Return Shipping

  • Wrong item received
  • Items damaged during shipping
  • Our processing errors

Customer Pays Return Shipping

Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for physical items within 30 days of delivery:

  • Same Item: Exchange for same item in different condition
  • Different Item: Exchange for item of equal or lesser value
  • Price Difference: Refund or charge for price difference
  • Shipping: Customer pays shipping for exchanges
