Shipping & Delivery

Fast, reliable delivery across Australia

Digital Game Delivery

Digital game codes are delivered instantly via email after your payment is confirmed. You'll receive:

  • Game activation code
  • Download instructions
  • Platform-specific setup guide
  • Customer support contact information

Delivery Time: Instant (within 5 minutes of payment confirmation)

Physical Game Delivery

Physical games and merchandise are shipped from our warehouse in Sydney, NSW.

Standard Shipping

  • Cost: $5.95 flat rate
  • Delivery Time: 3-7 business days
  • Tracking: Included
  • Coverage: All Australian states and territories

Express Shipping

  • Cost: $12.95 flat rate
  • Delivery Time: 1-3 business days
  • Tracking: Included with SMS updates
  • Coverage: All Australian states and territories

Free Shipping

Free standard shipping on all orders over $50.00

Shipping Zones & Delivery Times

Metropolitan Areas

  • Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide: 1-3 business days (Express) / 3-5 business days (Standard)
  • Canberra, Darwin, Hobart: 2-4 business days (Express) / 4-6 business days (Standard)

Regional Areas

  • Regional NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA: 3-5 business days (Express) / 5-7 business days (Standard)
  • Remote Areas: 5-7 business days (Express) / 7-10 business days (Standard)

Order Processing

Orders are processed in the following order:

  1. Order Confirmation: Email sent immediately after order placement
  2. Payment Processing: 1-2 hours (digital orders) / 24 hours (physical orders)
  3. Order Preparation: 1-2 business days for physical items
  4. Shipping: Items shipped via Australia Post or courier service
  5. Tracking: Tracking number sent via email

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Email confirmation with tracking number
  • Link to track your package online
  • SMS updates (Express shipping only)
  • Estimated delivery date

You can also track your order by logging into your account or contacting our customer service team.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any delivery issues:

  • Package Not Received: Contact us after 10 business days (Standard) or 5 business days (Express)
  • Damaged Package: Take photos and contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  • Wrong Address: Contact us immediately if you notice an error
  • Delivery Attempts: Australia Post will attempt delivery 3 times before returning to sender

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship physical items within Australia. Digital game codes are available worldwide.

For international customers, we recommend:

  • Purchasing digital game codes (available worldwide)
  • Using a freight forwarding service for physical items
  • Checking with your local game retailers for physical copies

Holiday Shipping

During peak holiday periods (Christmas, Easter, school holidays), delivery times may be extended by 2-3 business days.

We recommend placing orders early during these periods to ensure timely delivery.
